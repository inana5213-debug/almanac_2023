Mamoru Nagase closed the door for Saga, picking up his 1st save of the year.

Masaharu Kuroki dealt the Clippers a blow in the bottom of the fourth. Batting with the bases loaded and Saga leading 1-0, Kuroki, who signed a 5-year contract worth $97,000,000 on 12/18/2021, hit a first pitch slider for a run-scoring single.
