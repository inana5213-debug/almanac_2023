Yoshii Hits 3 home runs in Marlins Win
Behind a fine performance from Takaharu Yoshii, the Chiba Marlins knocked off the Shizuoka Rangers, 8-3. Yoshii slugged 3 home runs, while adding 5 RBI and scoring three times for the Marlins. Seiji Kobayakawa was the winner. Over 6 innings, he allowed 3 runs on 7 hits, with 2 walks and 6 strikeouts. The win gives Chiba a season record of 11-11.

Yoshii had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the sixth. On a 1-1 curveball from Nagateru Hasegawa, he banged a 2-run home run to right-center, giving the Marlins a 5-3 lead. It was the 4th home run of the year for Yoshii.

"We came out aggressive," said Marlins manager . "We came out swinging the bats."
