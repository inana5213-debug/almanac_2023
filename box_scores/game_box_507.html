The Nagano Grizzlies shut out the Akita Rams 6-0 today behind the strong pitching of Tomoki Yamauchi. Yamauchi surrendered only 1 hit in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 5.

Following the last out, Yamauchi appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Akita manager , who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Yamauchi's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Rams skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Grizzlies press conference, Yamauchi confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Yamauchi said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Nagano manager added.
