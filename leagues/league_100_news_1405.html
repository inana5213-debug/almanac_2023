Thrilling Win Pushes Tokyo to Next Round
Friday, September 16th , 2022
The Tokyo Mets were counting on Koki Kudo to put forward a strong performance in the League Championship at Shikishima Stadium. He certainly delivered.

With a solid 6 innings, Kudo earned MVP honors as his team beat the Gunma Rockies 5-1 to advance to the Japan Championship.

"I tried to just think of it as any other game," said the Tokyo starter. "But if we lose, we go home and I'd be lying if I said that didn't keep crossing my mind. That's motivation, for sure."

Kudo also had 2 strikeouts, while allowing 5 hits, no walks and 1 run.

While he wasn't willing to concede that the Mets are a superior team overall, Gunma manager gave credit to Tokyo for putting their best foot forward in a one-game format.

"They beat us straight up, that's all there is to it," he said.

The Tokyo Mets don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Kochi Diamondbacks - Shiga Galaxy series. That series has not started yet. The Japan Championship schedule will be announced after the League Championship finishes.
