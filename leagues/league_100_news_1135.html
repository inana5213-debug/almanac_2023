Kagoshima, Tottori Trade Made
Saturday, July 2nd , 2022
Earlier today, officials from the Kagoshima Steelers and the Tottori Saints announced that they had finalized negotiations on a trade between the two teams. The deal will send 24-year old minor league 3B Hiroaki Saito, 24-year old minor league 1B Yoshiharu Tashima, 19-year old minor league RF Junzo Takayama, and 21-year old minor league RF Shunsuke Yamamoto to Tottori in exchange for 28-year old LHP Tsuneteru Tanaka.


In 13 starts this season Tanaka has compiled a 5-7 record and a 1.95 ERA.
