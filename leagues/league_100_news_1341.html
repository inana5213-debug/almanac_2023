HNL Player of the Week Goes to Yoshii of Chiba
Monday, September 5th , 2022
A battle-tested Takaharu Yoshii declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 34-year-old Chiba right fielder collected the Higashi Nippon League Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .750 average, 3 hits in 4 at-bats, 3 home runs, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

This season Yoshii is batting .347 with 26 hits, 4 home runs, 15 RBIs, 8 walks, .410 OBP and has scored 13 runs. He has played in 22 games.
