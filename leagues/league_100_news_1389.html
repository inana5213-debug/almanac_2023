Nagano Advances to Division Match
Monday, September 12th , 2022
After their 6-5 win today over the Fukui Angels, the Nagano Grizzlies will advance to the Division Match.

Despite coming out on the losing side, the series MVP award went to Fukui right fielder Masahito Fukunaga. Fukunaga told a reporter from the Fukui Times that he was disappointed in the loss, and hopes to be back next year competing for the Japan Championship

Nagano won the series 1-0. The Nagano Grizzlies will play against the Gunma Rockies in the next round. Gunma had a bye in the Wildcard Match. The Division Match schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Match finishes.
