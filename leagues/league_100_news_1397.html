Yoshimura Raises Awareness at Local Animal Shelter
Wednesday, September 14th , 2022
Rockies left fielder and noted animal rights activist Go Yoshimura appeared at a local animal shelter today. Yoshimura visited the shelter to help call attention to the cause of pet adoption. "These animals desperately need to be taken in by loving families," said the popular Gunma player as he cradled a small puppy. "Many of these little guys were unwanted and abandoned. I just want to use my status in the community to help find good homes for them." Yoshimura, who is a member of PETA, has long been a spokesman for the organization and is a noted advocate for animal protection.
