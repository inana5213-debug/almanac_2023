Saturday, October 1st , 2022
It doesn't happen often that a reliever is the best rookie performer, but this season Kanagawa pitcher Kazuo Miyamoto earned the Higashi Nippon League Rookie of the Year Award for 2022.

The 26-year-old relief specialist for the Skyhawks made 18 relief appearances, tossing 27 innings with 19 strikeouts and 7 walks. Miyamoto compiled a 2-0 won-lost mark and a 0.67 ERA. The Kanagawa ace saved 0 ballgames and held opponents to a .176 average.

He received 39 first place votes out of a possible 48. Tetsuhisa Takahashi of the Chiba Marlins finished second in voting, while Toshikatsu Miyamoto of the Nagano Grizzlies finished third with 7 first place votes.
