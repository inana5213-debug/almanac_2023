Twins, Brewers Make Deal
Saturday, July 30th , 2022
The Twins and Brewers pulled the trigger on a deal that will see 33-year old 3B Masao Tomita and 24-year old minor league RF Michinobu Mukai head to Yamaguchi in exchange for 36-year old SS Shota Kawamoto. A source close to Mie officials said, "The teams spent long hours coming to the agreement and in the end, both agreed the deal should help both clubs."


Thus far this year Tomita is hitting .250 with no home runs, 9 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

This season in 10 games, Kawamoto has hit at a .333 clip with 3 hits, no home runs, 1 RBIs and 2 runs scored.
