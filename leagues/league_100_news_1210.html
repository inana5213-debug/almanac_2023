Kondo Swats Record-Breaking Home Run
Saturday, July 30th , 2022
Home run number 11 shot off his bat like a bullet, soared into the sky, and then disappeared into the stands today at Shikishima Stadium, breaking the single-season record held previously by Toshihiko Takahashi. Now it belongs solely to Shunta Kondo of the Gunma Rockies. His manager praised Kondo for his work ethic. "Shunta is totally focused on what he does, from hitting to running down the line. He doesn't go through the motions."

Shunta Kondo flied out in the 1st and homered off Yoshiyuki Yoshida in the 3rd.

Kondo's current batting average stands at .361 and he has chalked up 11 home runs and 22 RBIs.
