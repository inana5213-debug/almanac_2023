Gunma Crowned Kings of the Higashi Nippon League Koshinestu
Sunday, August 21st , 2022
While it remains to be seen what the playoffs will bring for the Gunma Rockies, the club took the first step by clinching the Higashi Nippon League Koshinestu title.

"It's exactly where we wanted to be and where we talked about being during spring training," said reliever Masatoshi Yagi. "There's not a guy in the room that doesn't believe in this team. We're all pulling in the same direction right now."

It was the team's 1st division title and they will attempt to capture the franchise's 1st Nippon Professional Baseball championship.

"The end goal hasn't changed - win the Japan Championship," said the team manager. "We think we've got the team to do it."
