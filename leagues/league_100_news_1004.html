Yoneda Boosts Hit Streak to 20 Games
Saturday, May 28th , 2022
Chalk up another one for Tokyo Eagles shortstop Tadaatsu Yoneda. He now has hit in 20 straight games. Yoneda went 1-for-3 today's game at Tokyo Citi Field but saw his club defeated 6-0 by the host Tokyo Mets.

"Playing on the road always makes it harder to get hits and to get wins," Eagles manager said after the game. "We got one of the two, but unfortunately it was the wrong one."
Tadaatsu Yoneda struck out in the 2nd, struck out in the 5th and singled in the 8th.

Yoneda is hitting .350 this year with no home runs, 3 RBIs and 4 runs scored. He has 14 hits in 40 at-bats, no walks and a .350 on-base percentage. Yoneda has played in 9 games.
