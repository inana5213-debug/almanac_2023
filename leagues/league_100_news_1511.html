Eagles, Clippers Make Deal
Sunday, December 11th , 2022
Tokyo and Tokushima have agreed in principle to a trade. The Eagles will send 18-year old minor league 2B Koji Minami, 24-year old minor league C Gohta Kawai, 24-year old minor league 2B Kento Mabuchi, and 24-year old minor league 2B Koji Taniguchi to the Clippers for 38-year old LF Kazumasa Yamashita. The deal is expected to be finalized shortly and the clubs will be introducing their new players to the press when the paperwork is complete.
