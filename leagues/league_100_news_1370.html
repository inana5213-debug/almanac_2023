Expos Win Nishi Nippon League Kinki Crown
Sunday, September 11th , 2022
Equal parts jubilation and relief swept over the Osaka Expos clubhouse tonight. The team captured the Nishi Nippon League Kinki crown, by the slimmest of margins.

"We're happy, excited, overjoyed, relieved, anxious - a lot of emotions going through our heads," said catcher Shigeru Kudo. "It's really hard to describe the wave of feelings you get going through a pennant race like this."

The Expos can now put the pennant push behind them and focus on the postseason, where the club will enter the bid for the Japan Championship with a 17-6 record.

It is the 2nd time the Expos have won the division in their team history and they are seeking a 2nd Nippon Professional Baseball championship.
