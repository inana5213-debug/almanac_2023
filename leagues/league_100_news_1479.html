SP Yoshimoto Signs Extension with Osaka
Sunday, November 6th , 2022
The Expos inked a deal with 30-year-old Norikazu Yoshimoto today signaling that it not only wanted him on the field but in the clubhouse.

Publicly, Norikazu Yoshimoto has spoken highly of Osaka, so the extension wasn't a total surprise.

Yoshimoto will draw $76,000,000 over the next 3 years.

In his career, Yoshimoto has crafted a 23-10 mark with a 2.44 ERA and tossed 288 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .217 against him and he has 236 strikeouts.
