Colts Send Miki to Nagasaki
Tuesday, November 1st , 2022
In an effort to add some depth to its roster, the Shimane Colts have swapped 19-year old minor league LHP Toshinori Yamamoto and 22-year old minor league C Hiromitsu Miki to the Nagasaki Titans for 26-year old 3B Tadaomi Nakagawa. The Colts GM told reporters, "We saw a chance to upgrade our ball club and took it."
