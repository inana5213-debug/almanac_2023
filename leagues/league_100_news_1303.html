Division Remains Up for Grabs, but Shiga in Postseason
Sunday, August 28th , 2022
Shiga Galaxy players hooted and hollered with excitement today when the club made it official - they've secured a playoff spot.

The Nishi Nippon League Kinki title is not yet secured, but the club will be going to the postseason regardless, on the strength of their current 15-6 record.

"Proud of the guys, proud of our effort," said reliever Riichi Takahashi. "These guys are hungry to bring the 1st Japan Championship to our franchise."
