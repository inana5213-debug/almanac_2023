Akita Rams, Skyhawks Agree to Trade
Friday, July 1st , 2022
Newspapers in Akita and Kanagawa reported that the Rams have reached a deal sending 31-year old RHP Ren Maeda to the Skyhawks for 19-year old minor league 2B Shogo Fuse and 23-year old minor league 1B Takashi Umeda.


In the 2022 season Maeda has worked 9 innings in 8 relief appearances and earned 3 saves. He has dished out 6 strikeouts and walked 2 with a 1-2 mark and a 5.00 ERA.
