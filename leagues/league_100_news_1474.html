Kagawa Trades Kobayashi for Kanagawa's Nagata
Monday, October 31st , 2022
The Baseball News Network is reporting that Kagawa and Kanagawa have agreed in principle to a swap that will send 30-year old RHP Katsutoshi Kobayashi to the Skyhawks with 19-year old minor league 3B Shunya Nagata joining the Pirates. Kagawa general manager Joe Unknown would not confirm the trade, but a team representative close to the negotiations said the deal is official and is now only awaiting approval from the league.
