Yoshimura Collects Player of the Week Award
Monday, August 8th , 2022
At the age of 27, Go Yoshimura might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Gunma left fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .800 batting average. For his efforts Yoshimura collected the Higashi Nippon League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 4 hits in 5 at-bats, 1 home run and 2 RBIs last week in 1 game.

This season Yoshimura has banged out 22 hits in 46 at-bats for a .478 average with 2 home runs. He is getting on base at a .510 pace and his totals also include 12 RBIs and 7 runs scored.
