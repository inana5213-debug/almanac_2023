Thrilling Win Pushes Shiga to Next Round
Saturday, September 17th , 2022
The Shiga Galaxy's Japan Championship dreams are still alive, as they've ousted the Kochi Diamondbacks in the League Championship, by a score of 2-1.

Kochi Ballpark played host to the one-game showdown, as Shiga was paced by the strong pitching of Susumu Noguchi, who was named MVP.

He threw 7 innings, with 3 strikeouts, while allowing 6 hits, no walks and 1 run.

"It won't be the last time we see Kochi in the playoffs, I'm sure of that," Noguchi said. "We're happy to come out of it with the win."

The Shiga Galaxy will play against the Tokyo Mets in the next round. Tokyo won their series against the Gunma Rockies. The Japan Championship schedule will be announced after the League Championship finishes.
