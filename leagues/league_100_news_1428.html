Mets Star Wins Sawamura Award
Sunday, October 2nd , 2022
Mets ace Koki Kudo is the latest Higashi Nippon League Sawamura Award trophy winner. The Tokyo star shined on the diamond time and again this year and finished with an outstanding 13-5 record.

In 23 starts he tossed 152.2 innings, posted 134 strikeouts and sported a 2.18 ERA. Opposing batters hit .221 when Kudo was pitching.

He received 31 first place votes out of a possible 48. Kaito Nishizaki of the Fukui Angels finished second in voting with 12 first place votes, while Masaaki Kadokura of the Hokkaido Vikings finished third with 5 first place votes.
