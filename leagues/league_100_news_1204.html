Cardinals Swap Matsumoto to Mets for Fukutomi
Saturday, July 30th , 2022
It likely won't turn either team into an instant contender or provide a huge franchise shakeup, but the Fukushima Cardinals and Tokyo Mets finalized a trade today. Fukushima will send 30-year old 3B Nobuo Matsumoto to the Mets, while the Cardinals will receive 24-year old minor league RF Katsumi Fukutomi and 19-year old minor league RF Kazuaki Kitsugi.

"We think this is a deal that will help out both teams and we're glad to get it done," Cardinals general manager Joe Unknown said.

The trade is expected to be officially completed later today with the players joining their new ballclubs by the end of the day.


Thus far this season Matsumoto is hitting .370 with 3 home runs, 7 RBIs and 7 runs scored. He has 17 hits in 46 at-bats and has played in 12 games.
