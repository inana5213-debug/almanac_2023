NNL Starter of the Month Goes to Yoshimoto
Wednesday, June 1st , 2022
In May Osaka starter Norikazu Yoshimoto mystified Nishi Nippon League hitters and carried off the Pitcher of the Month honors by chalking up a 3-0 record in 4 starts.

The 29-year-old Yoshimoto added 33 strikeouts in 26 innings and 1 walk to his season record and compiled a 1.04 ERA. Opposing batters hit .122 against him.
