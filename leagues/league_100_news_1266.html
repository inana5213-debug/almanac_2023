Shimada Wins NNL Player of the Week
Monday, August 15th , 2022
A battle-tested Hideyori Shimada declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 36-year-old Shimane third baseman collected the Nishi Nippon League Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .800 average, 4 hits in 5 at-bats, 1 home run, 2 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

To date, Shimada has collected 17 hits for a .243 average. He has posted 5 home runs, scored 10 runs and driven in 13 runs.
