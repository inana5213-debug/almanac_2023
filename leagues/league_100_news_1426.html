Nishi Nippon League Best Rookie Named
Saturday, October 1st , 2022
All year long Toshikuni Watanabe was the anchor of the Shimane bullpen. He made 20 appearances, had 5 save opportunities and nailed down 5 of them. The voters fell for him hook, line and sinker and gave him the Nishi Nippon League Rookie of the Year Award for 2022.

Watanabe was 2-0 with a 0.41 ERA. He hurled 22 innings, yielded 18 hits and fanned 21 batters. Other clubs hit .231 against him.

He received 41 first place votes out of a possible 48. Yoshinobu Yokota of the Hyougo Astros finished second in voting, while Tetsuya Soma of the Mie Twins finished third with 4 first place votes.
