Tokyo Captures Higashi Nippon League Kanto Crown
Sunday, August 21st , 2022
Tokyo Mets fans have reason to celebrate today, as their franchise is the class of the Higashi Nippon League Kanto.

With their record standing at 16-4, the club officially wrapped up the division title today, for the 2nd time. The team manager said everyone on their roster is looking forward to the postseason and trying to capture the club's 1st championship.

"That's what you dream about your whole life," he said.

Reliever Tadamichi Kaneshiro added that it's a great feeling to win the division, but said ultimately, a Japan Championship victory is what will mark success for the club.
