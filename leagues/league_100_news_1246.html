Setback for Diamondbacks and Sumiaki Hayashi
Sunday, August 7th , 2022
The Kochi Diamondbacks plan to take it slow with closer Sumiaki Hayashi, knowing that his recovery from an oblique strain would likely include a few bumps in the road. A team spokesman told BNN, "Sumiaki has up days and down days. It's still a process. He was feeling really good and then he had a step back. The doctors say it will be about 4 weeks before he can be activated."

In 2022 Hayashi has totaled 1 relief appearance and saved 0. His record is 1-5 with 61 strikeouts in 82 innings and a 3.29 ERA.
