Expos Headed to the Playoffs
Sunday, August 28th , 2022
The Osaka Expos celebration was a bit downplayed today, as the team officially secured a playoff spot.

First Baseman Fumiya Nakamura said his club expected to have got to the playoffs and thus, it's not as exciting as it will be when the team wins the Nishi Nippon League Kinki or advances to the Japan Championship.

The Expos record currently sits at 15-6 and they will be vying for the 2nd Nippon Professional Baseball championship in franchise history.
