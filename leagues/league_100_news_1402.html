Thrilling Win Pushes Shiga to Next Round
Thursday, September 15th , 2022
Shinji Takeoka said it was "cold comfort" to be named MVP in a losing effort, with his team ousted from the Division Match.

The Miyazaki Aces player had a standout performance against the Shiga Galaxy, but it still wasn't good enough to get the win, as his club fell 6-2 in the contest played at Miyazaki Fields.

"It's a sick feeling to get MVP and still not win the game," he said. "It really doesn't ease the pain at all."

There was certainly no pain in the Shiga clubhouse after the game, as the team joyously celebrated its berth in the League Championship. Players hooted and hollered, with bear hugs and high-fives being exchanged, as a Japan Championship victory remained a very real possibility.

The Shiga Galaxy don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Osaka Expos - Kochi Diamondbacks series. That series has not started yet. The League Championship schedule will be announced after the Division Match finishes.
