Record-Breaking Strikeout for Kadokura
Saturday, September 3rd , 2022
An amazing season for Hokkaido's Masaaki Kadokura reached its climax today, as the Hokkaido Vikings's starting pitcher set the league record for most strikeouts in a single season with 149, passing the great Kaito Nishizaki. Fans at Tochigi Oriole Park gave him a standing ovation after the historic whiff.

This year in 22 starts, Kadokura has recorded 7 wins and absorbed 4 losses. He has pitched 144 innings, allowed 109 hits, struck out 149 and has a 2.19 ERA.
