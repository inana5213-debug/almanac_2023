Kawamoto Wins HNL Player of the Week Prize
Monday, August 1st , 2022
Veteran Taketo Kawamoto had a great week at the plate for the Grizzlies to capture the majority of votes for Player of the Week in the Higashi Nippon League.

The 33-year-old Nagano right fielder built a .750 average on the strength of 3 hits in 4 at-bats, 1 home run and 3 RBIs.

In the current season, Kawamoto has put up a .283 batting average with 17 hits, 3 home runs, 12 RBI and scored 10 runs.
