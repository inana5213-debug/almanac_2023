Miura Throws 11-0 Shutout
Saturday, April 2nd , 2022
The Chiba Marlins shut out the Yamanashi Blue Jays 11-0 today behind the strong pitching of Daisuke Miura. Miura surrendered only 4 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 6.

Following the last out, Miura appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Yamanashi manager Setsujiro Tanaka, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Miura's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Blue Jays skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Marlins press conference, Miura confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Miura said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Chiba manager Shuji Matsuda added.
