Akita Selected HNL Player of the Week
Monday, May 2nd , 2022
The 41-year-old Kanagawa Skyhawks first baseman might not have the spring he had in his step years ago, but the "old man" can still play the game. Ren Akita has been a reliable ballplayer, one who has worked hard to earn everything he's achieved and today he sits on top of the heap as he was named the Higashi Nippon League Player of the Week.

He went 3-for-4 to rack up a .750 average with 1 home run and 1 RBI this past week. Akita also scored 3 runs.
