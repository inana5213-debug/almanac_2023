White Sox End Yoneda's Streak
Saturday, July 9th , 2022
It was the best of both worlds for the Ibaraki White Sox today at Hitachinaka Field, as they defeated the Tokyo Eagles 2-1 and also snapped Tadaatsu Yoneda's 24-game hitting streak. The 33-year-old Tokyo shortstop failed to get a hit in 4 at-bats.

"Nothing personal against Yoneda, we just wanted to win the game," said White Sox skipper . "We did a great job keeping him off-balance and guessing."

When asked by reporters about his streak coming to an end, Yoneda frowned and said, "I won't lie and say that I'm not disappointed about it, but I'd feel much better if we had won."

So far this season Yoneda is batting .333 with no home runs, 3 RBIs and 4 runs scored. He has 20 hits in 60 at-bats, 1 walk and a .344 OBP. Yoneda has played in 14 games.
