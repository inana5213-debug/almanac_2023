Thrilling Win Pushes Aomori to Next Round
Monday, September 12th , 2022
The Aomori Braves were counting on Kazuhisa Ishii to put forward a strong performance in the Wildcard Match at Haruka Yume Stadium. He certainly delivered.

With a solid 6 innings, Ishii earned MVP honors as his team beat the Tokyo Eagles 4-2 to advance to the Division Match.

"I tried to just think of it as any other game," said the Aomori starter. "But if we lose, we go home and I'd be lying if I said that didn't keep crossing my mind. That's motivation, for sure."

Ishii also had 4 strikeouts, while allowing 9 hits, no walks and 2 runs.

While he wasn't willing to concede that the Braves are a superior team overall, Tokyo manager gave credit to Aomori for putting their best foot forward in a one-game format.

"They beat us straight up, that's all there is to it," he said.

The Aomori Braves will play against the Tokyo Mets in the next round. Tokyo had a bye in the Wildcard Match. The Division Match schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Match finishes.
