Diamondbacks on to Division Match
Tuesday, September 13th , 2022
Two seasons came down to a single game in the Wildcard Match.

In the end, the Kochi Diamondbacks took the 12-4 win over the Hyougo Astros and advanced to the Division Match.

"You don't want to put too much pressure on, but in the end you have to get it done in this game," said third baseman Katsuaki Matsuda, who led his team to the win, going 2-for-2. "We got the job done and that's all that matters."

The Kochi Diamondbacks will play against the Osaka Expos in the next round. Osaka had a bye in the Wildcard Match. The Division Match schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Match finishes.
