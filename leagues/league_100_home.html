Saitama-Tokyo Trade: Hamada for Fujiwara
Sunday, January 1st , 2023
According to BNN there were mixed emotions by the players involved in the trade between the Saitama Bears and the Tokyo Eagles. As part of the deal, the Bears swapped 30-year old RHP Hiromi Hamada to the Eagles getting 19-year old minor league LF Koji Fujiwara and 23-year old minor league RHP Taiji Koga in return. The players indicated it was good to get a fresh new start in another city, but it was difficult to leave your friends on the old team.
Tokyo Players Star in New Commercial
Saturday, December 31st , 2022
The Tokyo Eagles have put out a new television commercial promoting the upcoming season.

The commercial features members of the team visiting a variety of Tokyo landmarks and playing pranks on one another.

"We had a lot of fun doing them and I think the fans will like the commercials too," said Motokazu Kobayashi, who is featured prominently in the ads.

Well-known team jokester Yasutoki Mori is also featured regularly in the ads, usually the one playing the pranks.

"I had fun making fun of my teammates, who were trying to pretend they were big-time actors," Mori said.

The commercials are set to start airing soon in the local market.
