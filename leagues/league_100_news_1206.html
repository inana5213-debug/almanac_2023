Shiga, Oita Close Deal
Saturday, July 30th , 2022
The Galaxy and Falcons pulled the trigger on a deal that will see 24-year old minor league 1B Morihiro Daidoji, 24-year old minor league LF Kunihiko Nogami, and 22-year old minor league RHP Nobuaki Takara head to Oita in exchange for 38-year old LF Shinji Hayashida. A source close to Shiga officials said, "The teams spent long hours coming to the agreement and in the end, both agreed the deal should help both clubs."


This year Hayashida has collected a .246 batting average to date with 15 hits in 61 at-bats, 7 RBIs, 3 home runs and scored 7 times. He has played in 16 games.
