Fukui-Ishikawa Announce Deal
Sunday, July 31st , 2022
The Angels and Pacers pulled the trigger on a deal that will see 23-year old minor league 2B Shigeyoshi Araki head to Ishikawa in exchange for 28-year old C Yamato Kajioka. A source close to Fukui officials said, "The teams spent long hours coming to the agreement and in the end, both agreed the deal should help both clubs."


For the year Kajioka is batting .500 with no home runs, 0 RBIs and no runs scored.
