Great Day for Yamaguchi -- 5 Hits
Saturday, April 9th , 2022
No matter what the Kagoshima Steelers threw at him today, Takashi Yamaguchi hit it. He easily handled Steelers pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Admirals to a 6-5 triumph at Kagoshima Ballpark.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Yamaguchi's quip, Steelers skipper said he "would take him up on that next time."

Takashi Yamaguchi singled in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.
