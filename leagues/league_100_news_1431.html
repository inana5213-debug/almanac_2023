Katsuaki Matsuda Chosen NNL's Best Player
Monday, October 3rd , 2022
What a way to finish off the year -- being honored with the 2022 Most Valuable Player Award in the Nishi Nippon League. It had already been a very special season for Kochi's Katsuaki Matsuda, but today's announcement really put the icing on his cake.

All season long Katsuaki Matsuda gave opposing pitchers fits by hitting .286 as he collected 24 hits, 6 doubles, no triples, 10 home runs and 18 RBIs.

He received 33 first place votes out of a possible 48. Yorinobu Kimura of the Wakayama Nationals finished second in voting with 7 first place votes, while Hiromitsu Matsuda of the Tottori Saints finished third.
