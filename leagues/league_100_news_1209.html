Ravens-Rams Deal: Yoshida for Yamamoto
Saturday, July 30th , 2022
The Ravens and Rams pulled the trigger on a deal that will see 23-year old minor league 3B Kyoichi Yoshida and 21-year old minor league C Motoshige Yamaoka head to Akita in exchange for 29-year old minor league RHP Shinnosuke Yamamoto. A source close to Miyagi officials said, "The teams spent long hours coming to the agreement and in the end, both agreed the deal should help both clubs."


This season in 15 starts Shinnosuke Yamamoto has a record of 2-4 with 59 strikeouts in 85.1 innings and a 3.16 ERA.
