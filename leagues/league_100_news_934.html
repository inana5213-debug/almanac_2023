Nagai Headed for Retirement
Sunday, May 1st , 2022
Kagawa second baseman Yoshito Nagai won't be back next year. The 38-year-old announced plans today to retire at the end of the 2022 campaign, saying that it was time to call it a career.

"I could probably play another year," Nagai said, "but I don't have that fire you need to play well. And I'm not going out there on those terms. It's a disservice to the Pirates and baseball in general. There are just too many aches and pains in this old body for me to continue playing."
