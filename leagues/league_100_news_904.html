Thrice Is Nice: Kondo Unloads 3 Homers
Saturday, April 23rd , 2022
Gunma Rockies right fielder Shunta Kondo had a magnificent game against the Toyama Rays today, rocketing three balls out of Toyama Dome to help the Rockies take the 10-0 victory.

"I got some good pitches to hit and I creamed them," he said after the game. "The first was a big, fat fastball and the last two were hanging curves. All three were mistakes."

For the game, Kondo went 3-for-6 with 6 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"There's a fine line between hitting home runs and line drives," Gunma manager said later. "Seems like Shunta found his home run stroke today."

Shunta Kondo hit a solo-shot off Shota Saito in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Shota Saito in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, struck out in the 6th, hit a three-run home run off Takeharu Miyagi in the 8th and struck out in the 9th.
