Diamondbacks Heading to League Championship
Thursday, September 15th , 2022
The Kochi Diamondbacks are on to the League Championship with a 4-0 win over the Osaka Expos.

"We always believed we would come out on top," said starting pitcher Daisuke Mori. "Nobody was ready to see the season come to an end."
