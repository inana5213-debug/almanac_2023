Nemoto Earns NNL Player of the Week
Monday, August 8th , 2022
Shuhei Nemoto of the Shiga Galaxy has just enjoyed one of the better weeks of his career. That fact was not lost on Nishi Nippon League officials, who named him Player of the Week.

Nemoto logged a .750 batting average for the Shiga Galaxy, racking up 2 RBIs and 1 home run.

This season Nemoto has hit 3 home runs and posted a .211 batting average.
