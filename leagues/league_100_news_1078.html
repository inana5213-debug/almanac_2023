Yoshimura Walks Away from the Game
Sunday, June 19th , 2022
Shinya Yoshimura is stepping away from the game of baseball at the end of the season. The Hyougo Astros player gave a brief explanation to reporters this afternoon.

"It's time to step away from the game and work on phase two of my life - world domination," the 40-year-old joked.

Yoshimura wisecracked for several minutes while reminiscing about high and low points of his career. At the end of the session, Yoshimura sent a personal message.

"I truly thank all you fans."
