Playoff Bound: Grizzlies Win Wild Card
Sunday, September 11th , 2022
The Nagano Grizzlies said an Higashi Nippon League Koshinestu championship was the goal, but they are still pleased to have won a wild card position.

"You'd rather go in there as the top team, but all that matters is we're in the playoffs," said the club's manager. "Once the postseason starts, everyone's got a chance."

Nagano right fielder Taketo Kawamoto said the members of the team are all incredibly focused on capturing a Japan Championship, which would be the club's 1st.

"We all want this badly," he said.
