HNL Announces Best Pitcher for July
Monday, August 1st , 2022
Koki Kudo really put his best foot forward in July with 4 wins and 1 loss and walked off with the Pitcher of the Month honors in the Higashi Nippon League. When he got the start for the Tokyo Mets, it was good news for his club, but bad news for the opposition.

In 33 innings of work, Kudo limited opposing batters to 30 base hits and a .246 batting average. He notched 20 strikeouts, issued 4 walks and had a 1.36 ERA.
