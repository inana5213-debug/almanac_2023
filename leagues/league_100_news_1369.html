Panthers Come up Short of Playoffs
Sunday, September 11th , 2022
That they came exceptionally close to qualifying for the playoffs will likely provide little solace for fans of the Aichi Panthers -- the team was mathematically eliminated from postseason contention today.

"I don't expect the fans to be happy with us," said Panthers catcher Mahito Araki. "We're not happy with ourselves."

The Panthers are still searching for their first playoff appearance. The team also missed qualifying for the postseason last season.
