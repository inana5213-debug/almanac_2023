Thrilling Win Pushes Tokyo to Next Round
Wednesday, September 14th , 2022
The Tokyo Mets received a strong pitching performance and have advanced to the League Championship, with a 3-0 win over the Aomori Braves in the Division Match.

Tokyo pitcher Koki Kudo was strong on the mound, leading his team to victory with 8 innings of work. He added 7 strikeouts, while allowing 4 hits, no walks and no runs.

Several Braves players sat in stunned silence on their bench as they watched Tokyo players celebrate the win in the middle of the field at Tokyo Citi Field.

"We're admittedly excited right now," Kudo said as he took a break from celebrating with his teammates. "To win a one-game playoff, it's thrilling."

The Tokyo Mets don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Gunma Rockies - Nagano Grizzlies series. That series has not started yet. The League Championship schedule will be announced after the Division Match finishes.
