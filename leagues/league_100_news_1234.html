Yamakura Visits Children at Cancer Treatment Center
Monday, August 1st , 2022
Rockies catcher Kazuki Yamakura took time out from his busy schedule today to visit a local children's cancer treatment center. Yamakura signed autographs and took pictures with many of the young patients. He even gave several of them caps, jerseys, and gloves. "Just seeing the smiles on these kids' faces, knowing what they've gone through, it just warms your heart", said a beaming Yamakura. When asked who his favorite player was, one little boy smiled broadly and exclaimed, "Kazuki Yamakura!"
