Thrilling Win Pushes Shiga to Next Round
Tuesday, September 13th , 2022
The Shiga Galaxy received a strong pitching performance and have advanced to the Division Match, with a 6-1 win over the Shimane Colts in the Wildcard Match.

Shiga pitcher Susumu Noguchi was strong on the mound, leading his team to victory with 7 innings of work. He added 4 strikeouts, while allowing 4 hits, 1 walk and 1 run.

Several Colts players sat in stunned silence on their bench as they watched Shiga players celebrate the win in the middle of the field at Shimane Ballpark.

"We're admittedly excited right now," Noguchi said as he took a break from celebrating with his teammates. "To win a one-game playoff, it's thrilling."

The Shiga Galaxy will play against the Miyazaki Aces in the next round. Miyazaki had a bye in the Wildcard Match. The Division Match schedule will be announced after the Wildcard Match finishes.
