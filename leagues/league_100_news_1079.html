Kajioka Honored by Higashi Nippon League
Monday, June 20th , 2022
At the age of 27, Motoyasu Kajioka might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Aomori third baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .750 batting average. For his efforts Kajioka collected the Higashi Nippon League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 3 hits in 4 at-bats, 1 home run and 3 RBIs last week in 1 game.
