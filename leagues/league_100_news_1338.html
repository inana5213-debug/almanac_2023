Shimane Captures Nishi Nippon League Chugoku Crown
Sunday, September 4th , 2022
Grown men turned into little boys in the Shimane Colts clubhouse, as the realization set in: the club had clinched the Nishi Nippon League Chugoku.

"We can relax a bit, for now anyway," said the club's manager. "I'm proud of every guy in the room for what we've done this season."

The club has a 12-10 record as they head towards the playoffs in search of their 1st Japan Championship championship. Closer Toshikuni Watanabe said the team is happy to clinch what is the club's 1st division title, but added that "we won't be happy until we're lifting that trophy."
