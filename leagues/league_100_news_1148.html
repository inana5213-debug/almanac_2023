Thursday, July 7th , 2022
Miyazaki third baseman, Eiichi Ando, filed for bankruptcy this morning in a Miyazaki court. According to the Chapter 7 filing, Mr. Ando's restaurant chain lost $5.5 million last year. Among his assets, the third baseman listed over 2 million in Rolex watches.

Eiichi has been quoted as saying, "What can I tell you? Those timepieces are the epitome of class. I can't say anything about my financial situation. Go through my lawyer."
