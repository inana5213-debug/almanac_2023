Matsui Comes To Training Camp Muscled Up
Saturday, March 12th , 2022
When training camp began this spring for the Angels, Jin Matsui walked into the clubhouse with a new mind and...a new body.

Over the offseason, Matsui added 10 pounds of pure muscle. Matsui attributes his new look to his intensive off-season workout regimen, which he compared to that of a football player. He also did a lot of biking around the Fukui area, and ventured out for hikes as well.

"I'm in the best shape of my life - I'm stronger and quicker than ever before." Matsui said with great confidence. "I'm ready to have the best year of my career," he said.
