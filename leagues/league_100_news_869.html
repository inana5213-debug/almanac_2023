Masumi Uesugi's Home Robbed
Monday, April 11th , 2022
Masumi Uesugi's home in Kanagawa has been robbed, the catcher has told media.

"They took pretty much everything, completely cased the place," said the 31-year-old grimly. "I'm not sure if it was someone who knew I was a pro ballplayer or just a random thing, but it feel terrible either way."

Thankfully, Uesugi said, his family wasn't home during the incident.

"We can replace possessions, but no one was hurt, that's the main thing."

Motoyasu Iwasa said morale around the clubhouse is low, but they are trying to band together to help Uesugi through the situation.

"We're all bummed out, but happy he's OK and hopefully they can catch the guy who did this," he said.
