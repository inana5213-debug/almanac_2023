NPB News: Free Agents File
Wednesday, October 12th , 2022
Free agency is upon us.

Teams, players and pundits who follow Nippon Professional Baseball will be thrust headlong into a whirlpool of intrigue and uncertainty. The free agent signing period will continue throughout the offseason as some clubs will go a long way toward sealing up a championship -- or a spot in the cellar.

The magic, the unpredictability of hot stove baseball, is fascinating to the fan; particularly the fan whose team comes out on top!
