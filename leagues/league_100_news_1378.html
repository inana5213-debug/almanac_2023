Suzuhara Has Spectacular .449 Season
Sunday, September 11th , 2022
It might look like a typo in this season's stat reports, but the .449 next to the name of Isami Suzuhara is no mistake. That's his batting average for the recently completed season. Suzuhara is the newest member of baseball's .400 club.

The Tokyo center fielder, who led the HNL in batting average with .449 in 2022, tried to downplay the achievement. "It's just a number," he said. Then he admitted with a grin: "Okay, it's a big number!"

That number puts Suzuhara in the record books with some of the game's great players. His other stats during this remarkable season include 31 hits, 6 doubles, no triples, 7 home runs, 26 runs and 18 RBIs.
