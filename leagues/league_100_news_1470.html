Chiba Sends Yoshida to Oita
Sunday, October 30th , 2022
24-year old minor league 1B Shinichi Yoshida will now be playing for the Oita Falcons and 30-year old RHP Masahiro Yoshida and $100,000 in cash will be suiting up for the Chiba Marlins. The trade was announced today at a press conference by club officials.

Oita will retain a portion of Yoshida's remaining contract.

Over his entire career Yoshida has worked 248.1 innings with 162 strikeouts en route to a 12-17 record. The 30-year-old also has a 4.02 ERA.
