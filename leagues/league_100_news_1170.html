Kondo HNL's Best of the Week
Monday, July 18th , 2022
At the age of 29, Shunta Kondo might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Gunma right fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .600 batting average. For his efforts Kondo collected the Higashi Nippon League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 3 hits in 5 at-bats, 2 home runs and 2 RBIs last week in 1 game.

For the season Kondo is currently batting .364 with 10 home runs and 22 RBIs. In 15 games he has reached base at a .432 rate and scored 19 runs to date.
