Izumi Releases Swing Album
Saturday, October 1st , 2022
When not playing baseball in Tokyo, Toshiro Izumi can usually be found singing in his swing band in his hometown of Kōka, Japan. "We first got together when we were in high school," Izumi said when asked about the origins of the band, "and this summer when we were playing at a local fair a producer saw us playing. He asked if we were interested in doing an album and and of course we said yes."

While music may not be as steady as baseball is, it's definitely a good thing to have options after retirement. You can now purchase the third baseman's album in stores across the nation.
