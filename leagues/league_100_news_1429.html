NNL Presents Top Pitcher Trophy
Sunday, October 2nd , 2022
This year there was no one better on the mound than Shinji Takeoka. Posting 6 wins and a 1.54 ERA was only part of the story for the Miyazaki superstar. For his outstanding performance in 2022 he was chosen as the Nishi Nippon League Sawamura Award winner.

From start to finish Takeoka never let up. In 22 starts he recorded 6 wins and 8 losses, 146.1 innings, 117 strikeouts, 23 walks, yielded 112 hits, and permitted opposing batters to hit .211 against him.

He received 46 first place votes out of a possible 48. Norikazu Yoshimoto of the Osaka Expos finished second in voting, while Masamichi Noguchi of the Hyougo Astros finished third with 2 first place votes.
