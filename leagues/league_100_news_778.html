Hideto Yamamoto Suffers Serious Injury
Sunday, January 9th , 2022
Hideto Yamamoto took up Crossfit in order to try to get in the best shape of his life. He ended up instead suffering a serious injury. The Fukuoka dodgers player has broken his leg during a Crossfit workout and is expected to be out of action for at least three months.

"I was doing some jumping squats for time and I just came down wrong, my leg buckled under the weight," the 24-year-old said. "I guess I'll just heal and go from there."

For his part, when told of the injury, the team manager Seiichi Taniguchi was none too pleased.

"He hurt himself doing Crossfit? Broken leg? Wow," he said. "I guess he shouldn't have been doing Crossfit, that much is obvious."

Yamamoto said that likely draws to a close his new fitness hobby.

"Back to the drawing board, I guess I'm done with Crossfit now," he said.
