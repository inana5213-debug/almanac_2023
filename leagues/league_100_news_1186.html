Ota Selected Top Performer in HNL
Monday, July 25th , 2022
In the twilight of his career, 35-year-old Tadanori Ota of Yamagata recaptured some of the old magic last week and came away with the Higashi Nippon League Player of the Week Award.

"I've been playing since I was a kid," Ota told reporters, "but you're never too old to feel the thrill of success."

Ota outpaced the rest of the league's hitters last week, reeling off 4 hits in 5 at-bats, no home runs, and 2 RBIs to stake his claim to the award.

Ota has a .224 average and a .274 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 13 hits, 1 home run and driven in 5 runs.
