Time's Up: Katayama to Retire
Sunday, April 3rd , 2022
After what he called a "long and rewarding" career in professional baseball, Shuntarou Katayama has announced he will retire.

The Yamagata Reds second baseman said it wasn't an easy decision to make, but this season will be his last and he's now "content" with his choice to walk away at age 37.
