The Tokyo Eagles are heading to the playoffs.

"Don't overlook us as a wild card team, that's all I'll say," said closer Takuya Aoyama.

That confidence and swagger has been characteristic of the Eagles this season, with many feeling they are better than their 16-7 record indicates.
