Shigeru Kudo Wins MVP in Nishi Nippon League Loss to Higashi Nippon League
Saturday, June 25th , 2022
Although his team came out on the losing end, nobody could deny Shigeru Kudo's performance in this year's all-star game.

The Nishi Nippon League All-Stars fell to the Higashi Nippon League All-Stars by a score of 6-4, but Kudo was still named the game's MVP.

"You would have liked to have won the game of course, but being named the MVP is a great honor," he said from his team's dressing room at Hyougo Astros Park.

In the contest, Kudo went 2 for 2.
