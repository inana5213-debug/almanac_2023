Suzuhara Selected HNL's Top Player
Monday, October 3rd , 2022
Isami Suzuhara has been around Nippon Professional Baseball enough to know a good hot streak when he sees one -- and he got to see one firsthand all year. For his standout run through the 2022 season, he's taking home the Most Valuable Player Award in the Higashi Nippon League.

The Tokyo center fielder put up a batting average of .449 with 6 doubles, no triples, 7 home runs and 18 RBIs.

He received 32 first place votes out of a possible 48. Shunta Kondo of the Gunma Rockies finished second in voting with 8 first place votes, while Motoyasu Kajioka of the Aomori Braves finished third.
