Tanaka Batters Akita for 5 Hits
Saturday, April 2nd , 2022
Ippei Tanaka had an answer for everything the Akita Rams threw at him today at Yamagata Ball Park. The Yamagata shortstop went 5-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Reds on top, 5-4.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Tanaka said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Ippei Tanaka struck out in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.
