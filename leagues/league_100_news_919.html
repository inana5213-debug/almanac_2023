Masumi Uesugi's Home Robber Arrested
Wednesday, April 27th , 2022
Masumi Uesugi was all smiles today as he told local reporters that the man who robbed his home recently has been arrested by local authorities.

"They caught him," he said. "I think I'm even going to be able to get some of my stuff back."

Uesugi said his Kanagawa Skyhawksteammates provided great support for him while he was struggling with this issue and he believes the team has come together and bonded as a result.

"Thankfully it's a happy ending and this low-life will get what he deserves," he said.

Motoyasu Iwasa said many of the guys on the team gave Uesugi a big hug when they heard the news.

"We're happy for him, it's been stressing him out, so it's nice to see it come to a conclusion," he said.
