Big 3-HR Day for Yamasaki
Saturday, July 9th , 2022
Fans are still buzzing after Tottori whipped the Ehime Tigers by the score of 8-0 today at Tottori Saints Ballpark.

Shingo Yamasaki smashed three home runs in the contest, a feat that would be a career highlight for most players and few fans ever see. In a tremendous performance, the first baseman bashed out 3 hits in 5 at-bats, had 6 RBIs and scored 3 times.

At the postgame press conference, Yamasaki said it was an "unbelievable" feeling to hit three home runs in a game.

"Getting one homer is special, and two is great," Yamasaki said. "Three is just... wow."

Shingo Yamasaki flied out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Riku Matsumoto in the 3rd, grounded out in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Minoru Shimizu in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off Shintaro Imada in the 8th.

Looking at his present stats, Yamasaki has compiled a .218 batting average in 14 games with a .295 on-base percentage, while hitting 3 home runs and driving in 7 runs.
