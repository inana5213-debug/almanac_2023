Kon Hauls in July's Best Rookie Award
Monday, August 1st , 2022
He might be a rookie, but he is a player. Takuya Kon of Nara certainly showed that this month with an outstanding 2-1 won-lost record and impressive 2.35 ERA. For that kind of performance Kon was named the Nishi Nippon League Rookie of the Month.

In July he started 5 games, tossed 30.2 innings, gave up 23 base hits, struck out 10 and walked 11. Opposing batters combined for a .213 average against him.
