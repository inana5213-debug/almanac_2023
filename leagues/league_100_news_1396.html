Thrilling Win Pushes Gunma to Next Round
Wednesday, September 14th , 2022
Gunma Rockies players, coaches and personnel swarmed the field at Shikishima Stadium tonight after the club posted a 7-1 triumph over the Nagano Grizzlies in the Division Match.

"We're not where we want to be yet, but any time you can survive a one-game elimination, you're feeling good," said Masahiro Kawakami, who had 4 hits in 4 at bats to pace his team's offence.

The club will look to continue playing strong in the League Championship.

"There's no reason this team can't go all the way, and everyone in this clubhouse believes that," said Kawakami.

The Gunma Rockies will play against the Tokyo Mets in the next round. Tokyo won their series against the Aomori Braves. The League Championship schedule will be announced after the Division Match finishes.
