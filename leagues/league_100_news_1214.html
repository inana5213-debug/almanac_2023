Brewers-Pirates Deal: Tanaka for Yamamoto
Sunday, July 31st , 2022
Yamaguchi and Kagawa announced today in a joint press conference that 33-year old RF Daiki Tanaka will be going to the Pirates in exchange for 23-year old minor league RHP Tomokazu Yamamoto and 24-year old minor league 1B Hirotoshi Arai. "It'll be exciting to join a new organization," one player said. "Some of us could use a fresh start." The players have each passed physicals and are expected to report by day's end.
