Masao Ryu, Attends Leadership Seminar
Tuesday, January 4th , 2022
Some say it's impossible to teach an old dog new tricks, but try saying that to 34-year-old Wakayama Nationals' left fielder Masao Ryu.

Ryu, a major league veteran, attended a Wakayama leadership conference last Sunday, hoping to hone his clubhouse skills.

"I'm getting up there in age now and I want to make sure I can serve as a mentor to some of the young guys coming up," said Ryu, known league-wide for his fostering of young players. "I want to be able to more effectively pass down the knowledge I've obtained to the younger guys, so we all get to see some better ball."
