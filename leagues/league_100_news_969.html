Miyazaki Whitewashes Ehime, Takeoka Winner
Saturday, May 14th , 2022
Before the game, Shinji Takeoka said he liked his chances. His instincts proved to be right. In a superb outing at Miyazaki Fields, the Aces pitcher whitewashed the Ehime Tigers 5-0.

"I just had a feeling it was going to be my day," Takeoka said in the clubhouse afterward. "My control was awesome. I could throw my fastball even when I was behind in the count."

The Miyazaki starter held Ehime scoreless, giving up 2 hits while registering 7 strikeouts and issuing 1 walk.

"We didn't have very many good at-bats," Tigers manager said in a postgame interview. "When that happens, you usually lose."

This year Takeoka has worked 49.2 innings with 35 strikeouts en route to a 2-2 record and 1.27 ERA.
