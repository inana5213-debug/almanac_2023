Someone Got Busted! Masanori Okuda Pays for Clubhouse Mishap
Wednesday, September 14th , 2022
Apparently the Hyougo kangaroo court really is in effect.

Youngster Masanori Okuda was fined $100 by judge Hiroya Kasashima for the seemingly harmless infraction of stepping on the team logo in the middle of the clubhouse floor.

"That's a big no-no," Kasashima said. "Come on, you should know better than that - walk around the logo!"

Other teammates agreed as Okuda hung his head in shame and grudgingly forked over the $100 bill.

"Let this serve as a lesson," added Kasashima. "Don't mess with the kangaroo court or you will pay!"
